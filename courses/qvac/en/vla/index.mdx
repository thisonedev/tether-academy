VLA (vision-language-action) models are the model class for robot control.

VLA (vision-language-action) models take a camera frame and a task description and produce an action chunk. The chunk is the next `chunkSize` timesteps of an `actionDim`-dimensional action vector for the robot's actuators. The models are trained on a wide range of manipulation tasks. The SDK includes two families: SmolVLA (small, fast) and π₀.₅ (larger, more capable). Both share the same `vla()` call surface.
